Roadmap
1
Discover Entrepreneur
Annual Competition

In Round 1, teams will develop and refine their ideas through a participatory design session (don’t worry if you don’t know what that is, we will show you). Thereafter, each group will present their idea by way of showing their designs accompanied by a slideshow to a panel of carefully selected and highly respected judges. The top 10 teams will move on to Round 2.

In Round 2, the 10 best teams will receive valuable entrepreneurship training through the one day TuksNovation Business Bootcamp. After the training, the teams will be expected to put together a commercialisation strategy and business plan built upon the idea they presented in round 1. These will again be evaluated by expert judges who will select the top 3 teams.

2
Develop Prototype
The development of a working prototype for each of the top 3 groups from that year’s competition will be funded and facilitated:

  • PMs from Informatics Design Labs (IDL) will sit with winning entrepreneurs to set up project plan (phases, milestones, deliverables).

  • PMs will recruit suitable student techies (engineers, software programmers, information designers, etc.) to make up prototype development teams.

  • Teams are briefed and development of the 3 prototypes for that year is completed in 5 to 8 months.

3
Business Development
Now equipped with their functional prototypes, student entrepreneurs are mentored by experts and volunteer MBA students from GIBS Business School.

  • Company registration.

  • Acquisition of further funding (if necessary).

  • Further development of product and surrounding business structures.

  • Take product to market
